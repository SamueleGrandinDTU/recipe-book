# Rosty recipe

## Description

Rosty is a delicious dish from the Italian and Spanish cuisine, easy and tasty, fast to prepare and with a low price weight on your shopping cart!

## Ingredients

- **4** Potatoes, medium size
- **1** tsp of salt
- **round of oil**

## Instructions

1. Grate the potatoes and squeeze them to remove the water in escess
2. Heat up the oil on a pan
3. Mix potatoes and salt, then cook them on the pan

Serve it on a dinner plate and cut your own slice!
